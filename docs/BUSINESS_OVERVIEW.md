# Studyspace Business Overview

## 1. Problem Statement
**Chaos in Academic Communication**
-   **Fragmentation:** Study resources, notes, and discussions are scattered across ephemeral WhatsApp groups, Telegram channels, and Drive folders.
-   **Lack of Verification:** Anonymous users in public groups often lead to spam, misinformation, or distractions.
-   **Ephemeral Nature:** Valuable discussions and shared resources in chat apps are lost over time or become hard to search.
-   **Resource Clutter:** Difficulty in finding specific notes, papers, or syllabus info when needed (e.g., during exams).

## 2. Our Solution
**Studyspace: A Verified, Structured Academic Ecosystem**
-   **Verification First:** Access is strictly tied to college domains (e.g., `@kiet.edu`). This ensures a safe, focused environment where every user is a verified student or faculty member.
-   **Structured Chat Rooms:** Persistent, topic-focused rooms (e.g., "DSA Study Group", "Cultural Fest") that organize discussions rather than losing them in a stream.
-   **Centralized Resources:** A dedicated repository for notes, syllabus, and papers, searchable and upvoted by the community for quality control.
-   **Sustainability:** A tiered system that allows free access for essential needs while offering premium features for power users and club leaders.

## 3. User Segments

### A. Verified College User (Core)
-   **Requirement:** Must sign up with a valid college email domain (e.g., `student@kiet.edu`).
-   **Capabilities:**
    -   **Full Access:** Can chat, post, vote, and upload resources.
    -   **Create Rooms:** Can create and manage study rooms (subject to plan limits).
    -   **Social:** Can follow departments and other users.

### B. Non-College / Guest User
-   **Requirement:** Users without a verified college email.
-   **Capabilities:**
    -   **Read-Only:** Can view public resources and syllabus.
    -   **Restricted:** **Cannot** join chat rooms, post messages, or create new content. This preserves the integrity of the college community.

## 4. Premium & Feature Plans for Verified Users

We operate on a "Freemium" model to keep basic education tools accessible while monetizing advanced usage.

| **Role** | Standard Student | Power User | Club Lead / Organizer |
| **Active Rooms** | **3 Rooms** | **8 Rooms** | **15 Rooms** |
| **Room Lifespan** | **1 Week** | **1 Year** | **1 Year** |
| **Messaging** | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Resource Uploads**| Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Private Rooms** | Yes | Yes | Yes |

### Plan Details
1.  **Free User (Default)**
    -   *Ideal for:* Quick study sessions, temporary project groups, or one-off discussions.
    -   *Constraint:* Rooms auto-expire after **7 days** to keep the server clean and encourage active, relevant usage.

2.  **Monthly Premium (₹49/month)**
    -   *Ideal for:* Active students running multiple study groups or societies.
    -   *Benefit:* Extended room limit (8) and significantly longer lifespan (1 year), ensuring communities can thrive throughout the academic year.

3.  **Yearly Premium (₹149/year)**
    -   *Ideal for:* Department leads, club presidents, or long-term community builders.
    -   *Benefit:* Maximum room capacity (15) and 1-year stability for a very low annual cost (effectively ~₹12/month).
